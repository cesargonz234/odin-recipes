Cod Acras

Seafood bites

Cod acras are carribean dish composing of flaked cod and a fritter batter. To eat them you grab them with your hand , they are a snack food.

Ingredients

  • Salt Cod 18oz
  • All purpose flour 7oz
  • Pinch of salt
  • water
  • Bay leaf
  • Olive oil 4tp
  • Cayenne papper
  • 2 shallots
  • 4 chives
  • 3 egg whites

How to make

  1. Place Salt Cod in water to remove salt
  2. Mix your all purpose flour with a pinch of salt and enough water to make a thick fritter batter.
  3. Cook the cod on a sauce pan gently for 10 min.
  4. Mix all your ingreients and deep fry.
