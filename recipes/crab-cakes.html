Crab Cakes

An Ocean treat

Crab cakes are a nautical delicacy. Everthing from the taste. to the look, and smell reminds one of the ocean no matter where they're eating one.

making crab cakes is a simple task, and if you follow the recipe at hand exactly, it's a guarentee that crab cakes will be your new favorite food

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup of mayonaisse
  • 1 tablespoon of parsley
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 8 saltine crackers
  • 1 lb crab meat

How to make

  1. In a bowl, mix your egg, mayonaisse, and spices. throw in your crackers as well.
  2. Add in your pound of crab meat, but mix do not mix exxcessivley so the clumps of crab are intact.
  3. Mold some patties with the crab mix
  4. Sautee them in butter and add a spritz of lemon toward the end.
  5. Enjoy them in a bun with lettuce, tomato, and tartar sauce.
