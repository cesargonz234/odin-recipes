Bulgogi Beef

A sweet and spicy adventure

This Bulgogi ground beef is a mix of comforting sweetness and a kick of exciting spice. This Bulgogi recipe will have you asking for seconds.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Green onion
  • onion
  • Chilli pepper: (red and green)
  • 3tb of soy sauce
  • 2tb of sugar
  • 2tb mirin
  • 2.5tb of oyster sauce
  • 2tb of garlic minced
  • 1tb of seasame oil
  • Black pepper for taste
  • Shitake mushroom

How to make

  1. First chop your green onion and seperate the the white and green onion.
  2. Then chop your onion, chilli pepper,and shitake mushroom
  3. Then make your bulgogi marinade with all your wet ingredients.
  4. Mix your marinade in the ground beef, and throw in your mushroom in the beef as well.
  5. Get a frying pan hot with 3tb of olive oil.Add your onion and the white part of your green onion.
  6. Wait until your onion browns a bit until you add your ground beef
  7. After the water renders out of the beef add your minced garlic. Do not stir frequently so that meat can brown.
  8. After the beef is all well browned turn off the heat and add your chill peppers.
  9. Serve on a bed of rice and garnish with the green part of your green onion.
